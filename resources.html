Resources

The purpose of this website is to share the experiences of people with housing choice vouchers and display the characteristics of the neighborhoods in which they live.

If you are experiencing housing instability, please contact the following resources:

  • Durham Housing Authority (919-683-1551)
  • Legal Aid of North Carolina (919-688-6396)
  • Community Empowerment Fund (919-797-9233)
